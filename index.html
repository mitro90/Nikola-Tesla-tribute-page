Short Tesla Biography

  • This Sebian-American inventor was born 10. July 1856.
  • He died aged 86 in New York City, United States, 7. January 1943.

His greatest inventions:

  • Alternating currents
  • Fluorescent bulbs
  • Electric motor
  • Tesla coil
  • Wireless communication
  • Radio
  • Remote control
  • X-ray

And much more claiming over the 300 patents...
